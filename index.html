Crazy Eights

Both players will recieve a opening hand of seven cards.
There is a card face up on the pile.
Your job is to put a new card on the pile that is either of the same suit, or value, as the card that is on the top of the pile.
An eight will act as a wildcard. Eights can be put down at any time, and can change the active suit to any suit you want.
The first player that gets rid of all of the cards in their hand wins.

Blackjack

You and the dealer will recieve two cards. You want to add up the values on each of the cards
Kings, jacks, and queens all have a value of 10.
An ace can have a value of either 1 or 11.
You cannot go over 21 or you will bust.
At the end of the round, add up the value of each players cards
The player with the higher valued hand wins

Matching

Flip over two cards.
If they have the same image on them keep them flipped over.
Otherwise flip them back.
Flip over all cards to win.

Go Fish

Start with 7 cards
Ask your opponent if they have a card with a matching value as one of yours in their hand
If they do, you both put those cards into the pile.
If not draw a card.
The first player to get rid of all the cards in their hand wins

War

Both players take a card from there deck and put it down.
The player with the higer valued card takes both cards.
If the values are tied, put out a card face down and face up.
Flip the face down card over. The owner of the higher valued card wins all cards.
